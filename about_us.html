About Us

The Story Behind Uncle Bob: A Taste of Taiwan in Malaysia

UncleBOB, established in 2002, has been delighting Malaysians with authentic Taiwanese street snacks for over two decades. Our signature offering, the crispy yet tender fried chicken, is meticulously prepared without skin and fat, ensuring a healthier indulgence for our customers.

With more than 290 kiosks and 680 locations nationwide, UncleBOB has become a beloved name among food enthusiasts across Malaysia.

At UncleBOB, we are committed to continuous improvement. Our dedicated R&D team in Taiwan consistently innovates to introduce new Taiwanese snacks, enhancing the culinary experience for our patrons.

Join us on this flavorful journey and discover why UncleBOB is synonymous with quality, taste, and tradition in Malaysia's vibrant food scene.
